==Step 1 - Specify the Content Ontology==

The Content Ontology is used to capture knowledge about some Learning Object. 

It describes the Concepts in the Object like the target and the prerequisite Concepts 
and also the relationships between those Concepts (Figure 1). 

[[File:/resources/jcropeditor/img/img1.png|border|Figure 1: Content Ontology Example]]


In the editor, Authors can import a predefined ontology, edit it, or create a simple hierarchy of classes from scratch.
